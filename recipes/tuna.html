Tuna

This tuna noodle casserole is the best with a classic creamy taste from my childhood.

The sauce is rich and creamy but not overwhelmingly heavy. The crunch from the topping and fresh bite from the parsley create the perfect balance.

Ingredient

  • Egg
  • salt
  • Butter
  • olive oil
  • mushroom
  • White onion
  • flour
  • Cans tuna
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add egg noodles and 3 teaspoons salt to the boiling water;
    3. Heat 2 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and add mushrooms
    4. Add sauce mixture to reserved noodles.
    5. Stir together bread crumbs, remaining 2 teaspoons oil, remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt, and paprika in a small bowl.
    6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and bubbly, 25 to 30 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
