Ground Beef

My cabbage roll casserole has all the ingredients of stuffed cabbage rolls.

Cabbage roll casserole is the saucy, satisfying 9x13 dish that will take your weeknight dinners from boring to bold.

Enjoy a casserole dish that combines seasoned ground beef, chopped cabbage, and fluffy white rice with tomato sauce and beef broth for a wonderfully cozy bite.

Ingredient

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 Â½ pounds chopped cabbage
  • (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
    3. Combine cabbage, tomato sauce, onion, rice, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Stir in cooked ground beef.
    4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Stir, re-cover, and bake until cabbage is tender and rice is done,
